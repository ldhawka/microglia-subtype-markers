Disease-associated sub-states share TREM2/APOE/GPNMB and overlap by design — score per sub-state, then roll up to the umbrella. The QC / Artifact-prone umbrella collects ribosomal/immediate-early/heat-shock signatures that are frequently ex-vivo dissociation artifacts (van den Brink et al. 2017 (10.1038/nmeth.4437); Marsh et al. 2022 (10.1038/s41593-022-01022-8)) — recognize and exclude, don't annotate.
